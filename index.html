What is Enso Journal?

Enso Journal is a private, AI-powered journaling app for iOS. The name Enso (円相) is a Japanese Zen concept: the circle — a symbol of wholeness, presence, and the uninhibited expression of the self. The app embodies that philosophy: a space to speak honestly, reflect deeply, and grow continuously.

Unlike passive note-taking apps, Enso is an active reflection partner. Each entry is analysed by GPT-5 in the context of your recent entries, emotional history, and psychological Soul Profile. Reflections become richer and more personalised over weeks and months — the longer you journal, the better Enso knows you.

Enso is built on a local-first privacy model. Your journals never leave your device to a server. They are sent temporarily to the OpenAI API to generate your AI reflection, then discarded. No cloud storage. No account required. No surveillance.

How it works

Three steps to self-understanding

Enso fits into your life with minimal friction and maximum depth.

01

Record

Tap the microphone and speak freely — there is no time limit. Enso transcribes your voice using OpenAI Whisper and polishes the text for clarity. Prefer typing? You can write instead. Your original audio is saved locally on your device.

02

Reflect

GPT-5 analyses your entry in the context of your last five entries, your latest weekly and monthly summaries, and your Soul Profile. It returns an empathetic opening, a three-part written reflection, and mood and tag analysis — all in your language.

03

Discover

As you journal, Enso builds a psychological Soul Profile identifying your core values, recurring struggles, emotional patterns, blind spots, and narrative identity. Weekly, monthly, and yearly summaries narrate your inner arc. Soul Art visualises it.

Privacy

Your thoughts stay on your device

Enso is built on a local-first architecture — a deliberate design decision, not a marketing claim. Your journal is the most intimate document you own. It should be treated accordingly.

On-device storage

All journal entries, summaries, Soul Art, and your Soul Profile are stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using iOS encrypted storage. Enso has no servers that hold your data.

No account required

You don't create an account to use Enso. There is no email address collected, no profile linked to you in any external system, and nothing to sign up for.

AI processing is transient

When you submit an entry, it is sent to the OpenAI API to generate your reflection. This transmission is temporary — your entry is not retained by Enso's backend or stored anywhere outside your device.

Biometric lock

Protect your journal with Face ID or Touch ID. Even if someone has physical access to your phone, your entries remain locked behind biometric authentication.

What is Enso Journal?
Enso Journal is a private, AI-powered journaling app for iOS. You speak or write your thoughts, and Enso delivers a deep, context-aware reflection powered by GPT-5 — drawing on your recent entries, emotional patterns, and psychological profile. How does AI reflection work in Enso?
When you submit a journal entry, Enso sends it to GPT-5 along with your last five entries, your latest weekly and monthly summaries, and your Soul Profile. The AI returns an empathetic opening that mirrors your emotional state, a three-part reflection exploring your thoughts in depth, and mood and tag analysis. Is Enso Journal private and secure?
Enso uses a local-first architecture — all journal entries, Soul Art, summaries, and your Soul Profile are stored exclusively on your device using iOS encrypted storage. Your data is never stored on Enso's servers. Entries are sent temporarily to the OpenAI API for AI reflection generation, then discarded. No account is required. What is Soul Art in Enso Journal?
Soul Art is a unique AI-generated artwork created from your psychological Soul Profile. Enso selects an artist style from a curated pool of 20 artists — weighted to match your profile attributes — then uses GPT-5 to translate your values, struggles, and identity into a visual prompt in that artist's style. What is a Soul Profile and when does it unlock?
A Soul Profile is Enso's AI-generated psychological portrait of you, synthesised from your journal entries. It identifies your core values, long-term goals, recurring struggles, emotional patterns, blind spots, key strengths, and your narrative identity — the story you tell yourself about who you are. The Soul Profile unlocks after 10 journal entries and deepens and updates as you continue journaling over time.
Does Enso Journal support voice journaling?
Yes. Enso is designed primarily for voice journaling. Tap record, speak freely with no time limit, and Enso transcribes your speech using OpenAI Whisper, polishes the transcript for readability, then generates your AI reflection. The original audio file is also saved locally on your device alongside the entry.

Unlike generic AI chat apps, Enso specialises exclusively in journaling with context that compounds across months and years of entries. Key differentiators: local-first privacy with no server data storage, voice-first design with Whisper transcription, GPT-5 context-aware reflections that reference your full history, Soul Profile psychological analysis, Soul Art generation, and Enso Advice — a weekly actionable suggestion derived from your real patterns.
